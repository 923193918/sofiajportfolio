Sofia Yumiko Jacobs is a third-year student at San Francisco State University. Her major is visual communications, but she was originally a fashion merchandising major. Sofia found an interest in graphic design after a content creator she followed began documenting their journey making flyers for local bands. Soon, they got popular enough to make flyers for popular bands, such as Type-O Negative, Slipknot, Billie Eilish, Rihanna, and more famous artists.

Sofia has only lived in San Francisco for about 3 years, but she is originally from Las Vegas, Nevada. Sofia's parents met in San Francisco, at their jobs, at the AAA on Hickey Boulevard (which is not that far from San Francisco State University). Sofia's parents moved to Las Vegas after her dad received a job opportunity, but it is interesting to know that she could have been born and raised in San Francisco. Growing up in Las Vegas was an interesting experience, since it is not a very kid-friendly place to live. Overall, she would say that she would not ever go back to live there again.

After living in San Francisco for 3 years, Sofia never wants to leave! She loves San Francisco so much because of how different it is from where she grew up. She loves how there is so much culture and community in San Francisco. San Francisco feels like a place where everyone has a space to exist, which is something that Sofia loves. Sofia's favorite places to go in San Francisco are Dolores, Hippie Hill, the Mission, Japantown, and Chinatown. Exploring the city with her friends is one of her favorite pastimes. Some more random facts about Sofia include: She is one sibling out of 3 (She is the youngest), She is mixed race (her mom is Mexican, and her dad is White and Asian), her favorite color is pink, and her comfort show is SpongeBob SquarePants.
