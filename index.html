My name is Sofia Yumiko Jacobs, and I am a third-year student at San Francisco State University. My major is visual communications, and I am hoping I will be able to find a graphic design job after I graduate. I found an interest in graphic design after a content creator I followed began documenting their journey being commissioned to make flyers for popular bands, such as Type-O Negative, Slipknot, Billie Eilish, Rihanna, and more famous artists. I find it interesting that you can get commissioned to make designs for artists; it is essentially being paid for being creative.

I have lived in San Francisco for about 3 years, but I am originally from Las Vegas, Nevada. My parents met in San Francisco, at their jobs, at the AAA on Hickey Boulevard (which is not that far from San Francisco State University). My parents moved to Las Vegas after my dad received a job opportunity, but it is interesting to know that I could have been born and raised in San Francisco. Growing up in Las Vegas was an interesting experience, since it is not a very kid-friendly place to live. Overall, I would say that I would not ever go back to live there again.

After living in San Francisco for 3 years, I never want to leave! I love San Francisco so much because of how different it is from where I grew up. I love how there is so much culture and community here. San Francisco feels like a place where everyone has a space to exist, which is something that I love. My favorite places to go in San Francisco are Dolores, Hippie Hill, the Mission, Japantown, and Chinatown. Exploring the city with my friends is one of my favorite pastimes. Some more random facts about me include: I am one sibling out of 3 (I am the youngest), my mom is Mexican, and my dad is White and Asian, my favorite color is pink, and my comfort show is SpongeBob SquarePants.
