My Top 5 Favorite Movies

Nowhere

Nowhere, 1997. Directed by Gregg Araki. Colorful wardrobe, strange dialogue, stylized sets, and interesting subject matters.

Rocky Horror Picture Show

Rocky Horror Picture Show, 1975. A cult classic, love the campiness of this film, costumes, and I love musicals!

Helter Skelter

Helter Skelter, 2012. Drama/thriller, highly stylized, amazing costumes, love the message.

Coraline

Coraline, 2009. Beloved movie growing up. A beautifully eerie stop‑motion. It took around 4 years to create this film!

Grease

Grease, 1978. A classic musical, amazing soundtrack, amazing costumes, love the retro aesthetic. Transports you to the 50's.

My Top 5 Favorite Characters

Elvira

Iconic character, known for her gothic style, beauty, and humour. Featured in movies/TV, even starring in her own, such as Elvira's Haunted Hills.

Creature from the Black Lagoon

One of the original Universal Monsters. The movie was released in 1954, considered a classic horror film.

Gir

Gir originates from the tv series Invader Zim, and he is the sidekick to Zim. Gir has a happy-go-lucky, head-in-the-clouds personality.

Hello Kitty

Hello Kitty was created by Japanese designer Yuko Shimizu in 1974. She grew in popularity rapidly, and still is an iconic character today.

SpongeBob SquarePants

Iconic main character from the popular Nickelodeon TV show SpongeBob SquarePants. He is known for his bubbly, funny, and big personality.
