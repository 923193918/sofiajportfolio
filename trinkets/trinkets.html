Monchichi

Monchichis originated in 1974 from the brand Sekiguchi Corporation. Monchichis were particularly popular during the 1980's, specifically in Germany. The name Monchichi is a combination of the french word "Mon" meaning my and "chichi" which is the sound that babies make when they suck their pacifier in Japanese.

Vintage trolls

Vintage troll dolls Vintage trolls were created by a Danish man, Thomas Dam, in 1956. They rose in popularity, specifically from the 60's til the 90's. Many companies took inspiration from Thomas Dam and made similar trolls, such as treasure trolls or Russ trolls.
